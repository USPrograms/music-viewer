Would you tell me how you feel

Past pretense, behind the mask

Share things that are occluded from my eyes?

Would you ever dare reveal

What goes on inside your mind

Tear down, just for a while, the disguise?

Frustration, delight,

Despondency, love, joy,

Anger, spite, contrition, pride, despair,

Shame, sadness, regret,

Stress, thankfulness, rage, fear,

Wouldn’t it feel good to lay it bare?

Will I ever get to see

What’s the truth that lies beneath

the smile and the polite “I’m doing fine”?

Will you ever dare to be

Your true self just for a while

You show me yours I might just show you mine

Frustration, delight,

Despondency, love, joy,

Anger, spite, contrition, pride, despair,

Shame, sadness, regret,

Stress, thankfulness, rage, fear,

Wouldn’t it feel good, every now and then,

Wouldn’t it be great to just lay it bare?
